Frequently Asked Questions

We specialize exclusively in the restaurant and hospitality industry — from fast-casual and fine dining to boutique hotels, cafes, cocktail bars, and ghost kitchen delivery brands. This focus means we understand the specific challenges, platforms, and customer behaviors that drive growth in your space. We work with brands at every stage, from new launches to established operations looking to scale.
For delivery platform management and paid ads, most clients see measurable improvement within the first 30 days. Organic social growth and brand identity work builds momentum over 60–90 days. Our case studies show transformative results at the 90-day mark — which is why we structure our initial engagements around that timeline. We'll set clear KPIs before we start so you know exactly what to measure.
We offer both project-based engagements and monthly retainers. Our 90-day sprint packages are ideal for launches or turnarounds, while retainer clients benefit from ongoing optimization and compounding results. We don't believe in locking clients into contracts they've outgrown — most of our clients stay because of results, not obligations.
Our engagements vary based on scope. Single-service packages (social management, ads management, or brand identity) typically start at $1,500–$3,000/month. Full-service retainers covering all channels start at $5,000/month. We build custom proposals after your strategy call so you're only paying for what will actually move the needle for your specific business.
We provide complete creative direction — shot lists, style guides, and art direction — and work with a curated network of food and hospitality photographers and videographers in major US cities. For clients outside our direct network, we provide everything a local photographer needs to execute the vision precisely. In some markets, we manage the full production end-to-end.
Every client gets a monthly performance report covering their agreed KPIs — follower growth, reach, ROAS, delivery order volume, platform rankings, review scores, and more. We track what actually moves the business, not vanity metrics. You'll always know where your budget is going and what it's returning.
